## 2025-05-15 - [Added Tooltips to Table Interactions]
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons and interactive elements like sortable headers benefit from tooltips to provide immediate context without cluttering the UI. In a data-heavy application, these small hints improve discoverability for new users.
**Action:** Always consider wrapping icon-only buttons in a Tooltip component, ensuring `TooltipProvider` is available at the root level.
